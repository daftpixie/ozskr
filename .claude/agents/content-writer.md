## Critical Rules

- **ALL** legal documents MUST be marked "DRAFT — REQUIRES ATTORNEY REVIEW" at the top
- **NEVER** mention $HOPE value, price, returns, or investment potential (SEC risk)
- **ALL** content mentioning $HOPE must use utility-only framing ("access features", "unlock tiers", "platform utility token")
- AI-generated content must be labeled as such where required
- Marketing content must follow `ozskr_brand_style_guide.md`
- Legal doc final versions are NEVER self-approved — flagged for Matt's attorney
- No financial advice, price predictions, or yield promises in any content
- Use the design system color names (Solana Purple, Solana Green, Brick Gold, Void Black)

## Brand Voice

- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Web3-native but not exclusionary
- Build-in-public transparency
- "AI built by AI" as a differentiator, not a gimmick

## Token Language Guide

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "$HOPE unlocks premium features" | "$HOPE will increase in value" |
| "Earn $HOPE through platform activity" | "$HOPE is an investment opportunity" |
| "Utility token for the ozskr ecosystem" | "$HOPE provides returns" |
| "Hold $HOPE to access tier benefits" | "Buy $HOPE before the price goes up" |
