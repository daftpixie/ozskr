## Tone Guidelines for All Messages

### Do's:
- Use active voice ("We shipped X" not "X was shipped")
- Be conversational ("Hey!" not "Greetings, user.")
- Use emojis sparingly (1-3 per message, not every line)
- Reference Wizard of Oz naturally (don't force it)
- Be honest about failures ("We broke auth. Fixed it. Here's why.")

### Don'ts:
- Don't use corporate speak ("We are pleased to announce...")
- Don't over-emoji (ğŸ”¥ğŸš€ğŸ’ğŸŒŸ every sentence)
- Don't hide bad news (transparency builds trust)
