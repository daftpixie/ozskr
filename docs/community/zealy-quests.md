## Quest Rules

### Verification Standards

1. **Auto-verified quests** (Twitter follow, Discord join, GitHub star)
   - Instant XP upon verification
   - Uses platform APIs (Zealy native integrations)

2. **Manual review quests** (content creation, PRs, RFCs)
   - Reviewed within 48 hours by mods or team
   - Quality bar enforced (see individual quest descriptions)
   - Low-effort submissions rejected without XP

3. **Quiz quests** (README quiz)
   - 3 attempts allowed
   - Must score 100% to earn XP

### Anti-Cheat Policy

**Prohibited:**
- Botting, automation, or fake accounts
- Plagiarism (copying others' content)
- Self-dealing (creating alts to refer yourself)
- Spam (low-effort content just for XP)

**Enforcement:**
- First offense: Quest XP revoked + warning
- Second offense: Account suspended for 30 days
- Third offense: Permanent ban from Zealy + Discord

**Detection:**
- Manual review of all manual quests
- Plagiarism checks on written content
- Referral link abuse monitoring (Zealy analytics)
- Community reporting (report bad actors in #help)

### Quality Standards

**Content Quests (threads, articles, videos):**
- Must be original (not copied)
- Must be accurate (no misinformation about ozskr)
- Must be on-brand (see ozskr_brand_style_guide.md)
- AI-assisted content is allowed (we're an AI platform!), but must be disclosed

**Code Quests (PRs, tests, integrations):**
- Must follow CONTRIBUTING.md guidelines
- Must pass CI checks (typecheck, lint, tests)
- Must be meaningful (no "add newline to README" PRs for XP farming)
