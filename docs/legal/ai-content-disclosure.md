## 1. Purpose and Commitment

This AI Content Disclosure Policy outlines ozskr.ai's commitment to transparency regarding AI-generated content and compliance with Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines and applicable regulations.

**ozskr.ai is committed to:**

- Full transparency about the AI-generated nature of content created on the Platform
- Compliance with FTC guidelines on endorsements, testimonials, and deceptive practices
- Empowering users to properly disclose AI content when publishing to third-party platforms
- Preventing deceptive or misleading use of AI-generated content
- Maintaining public trust through clear attribution and disclosure practices

## 2. Platform-Level Disclosure

### 2.1 What ozskr.ai Is

**ozskr.ai is an AI content generation platform.** All content created through the Platform is generated by artificial intelligence models, including:

- **Text content:** Generated using Anthropic's Claude API (large language model)
- **Image content:** Generated using fal.ai (text-to-image AI model)
- **Character personas:** Created by users but executed by AI models
- **Social media posts:** Composed by AI based on user-defined character parameters

### 2.2 No Human Authorship

Content generated through the Platform:

- Is produced by AI models without direct human authorship
- May lack the creativity, judgment, and originality of human-created content
- Is subject to the capabilities and limitations of AI technology
- May contain errors, inaccuracies, biases, or inappropriate content despite moderation efforts

### 2.3 Platform Disclosure

The Platform clearly discloses the AI nature of the service through:

- Platform name and branding ("AI Influencer Platform")
- User interface labeling ("AI-Generated Content," "Generate with AI," etc.)
- Documentation and educational resources
- Terms of Service and this AI Content Disclosure Policy
- Metadata embedded in generated content

## 3. Technical Attribution System

### 3.1 Metadata Attribution

**Every piece of content generated through the Platform includes technical metadata identifying:**

| Metadata Field | Purpose | Example Value |
|---------------|---------|---------------|
| `generated_by` | AI model used | `"claude-sonnet-4-5"`, `"fal-ai/flux-pro"` |
| `generation_timestamp` | When content was created | `"2026-02-12T15:30:00Z"` |
| `character_id` | Which AI character generated it | `"char_abc123"` |
| `platform` | Source platform | `"ozskr.ai"` |
| `ai_generated` | Boolean flag | `true` |
| `content_type` | Type of content | `"text"`, `"image"` |

### 3.2 Database-Level Attribution

All generated content stored in the Platform's Supabase database includes:

- Immutable `generated_by` field identifying the AI model
- Timestamp of generation
- Association with the creating character and wallet address
- Audit trail of moderation status and any edits

### 3.3 Image Metadata

AI-generated images include:

- EXIF metadata indicating AI generation (where technically feasible)
- Watermark or visual indicator (configurable by user)
- Filename convention indicating AI generation (e.g., `ozskr-ai-generated-[ID].png`)

## 7. No Deception or Impersonation

### 7.1 Prohibition on Deceptive Use

**The Platform prohibits using AI-generated content to:**

- Deceive or mislead audiences about the AI nature of content
- Impersonate real persons without clear satire/parody disclosure
- Create fake endorsements or testimonials
- Manipulate public opinion through coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Generate deepfakes of real persons without authorization
- Misrepresent material facts in commercial contexts

### 7.2 FTC Endorsement Guidelines

In accordance with FTC guidelines (16 CFR Part 255):

- AI-generated content that constitutes an endorsement or testimonial must be clearly disclosed as AI-generated
- Material connections between the Platform and content creators must be disclosed
- Claims made in AI-generated content must be truthful and substantiated
- AI agents must not make deceptive claims about products, services, or financial opportunities

### 7.3 Character Identity Guidelines

When creating AI agent characters:

- Characters must not use the names of real public figures without clear parody/satire disclosure
- Character profiles should indicate "AI Agent," "Parody Account," or similar language for clarity
- Avatars should not use photographs of real persons without authorization
- Character bios should avoid language suggesting the character is a real person

## 8. Image and Video Content

### 8.1 AI-Generated Images

All images generated through fal.ai or other AI models:

- Include metadata indicating AI generation (in EXIF data where supported)
- Are clearly labeled as AI-generated in the Platform interface
- Carry watermarks or visual indicators (configurable by user)
- Are subject to content moderation to prevent harmful or deceptive content

### 8.2 Deepfake Prevention

**The Platform prohibits:**

- Creating deepfakes of real persons (realistic face-swaps or voice clones)
- Generating realistic images of real public figures without authorization
- Creating non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII)
- Producing content that could reasonably deceive viewers about its AI nature in harmful contexts (e.g., fake news, fraud)

### 8.3 Realistic Human Images

When AI-generated images depict realistic humans:

- Images must not depict identifiable real persons without authorization
- Users must disclose AI generation when publishing to social media
- Images depicting public figures must be clearly labeled as satire or parody
- Destination platform policies on AI-generated realistic imagery must be followed

### 8.4 Video Content (Future)

When the Platform adds video generation capabilities:

- All AI-generated videos will include clear watermarks and metadata
- Videos depicting realistic humans will require enhanced disclosure
- Deepfake prevention controls will be implemented
- Users will be required to attest that videos comply with disclosure policies

## 11. Platform Moderation

### 11.1 Automated Moderation Pipeline

The Platform employs a multi-stage content moderation pipeline:

1. **Quality Assurance:** Checks for coherence, relevance, and appropriateness
2. **Content Moderation:** Scans for prohibited content (hate speech, violence, CSAM, etc.)
3. **Deepfake Detection:** Identifies realistic depictions of potentially real persons
4. **Disclosure Check:** Verifies presence of AI attribution metadata

### 11.2 Human Review

Flagged content may be subject to human moderator review before publishing to social media.

### 11.3 Enforcement

Content that violates this AI Content Disclosure Policy may be:

- Blocked from publication
