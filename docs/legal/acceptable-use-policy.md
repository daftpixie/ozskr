## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines prohibited content and conduct on the ozskr.ai Platform ("Platform"). This AUP supplements and is incorporated into our Terms of Service.

**By using the Platform, you agree to comply with this AUP.** Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination of access.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content

You may not create, generate, store, publish, or distribute content that:

### 2.1 Illegal Content

- Violates any applicable local, state, national, or international law or regulation
- Promotes, facilitates, or provides instructions for illegal activities
- Infringes intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or other proprietary rights)
- Violates privacy rights or misappropriates personal information of others

### 2.2 Harmful or Dangerous Content

- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** Any content depicting, describing, or promoting child sexual exploitation or abuse (zero tolerance — immediate permanent ban and report to National Center for Missing & Exploited Children)
- **Violence:** Content that incites, glorifies, or threatens physical violence or harm to individuals or groups
- **Self-Harm:** Content that promotes, encourages, or provides instructions for self-harm or suicide
- **Dangerous Activities:** Content providing instructions for creating weapons, explosives, or dangerous substances

### 2.3 Hate Speech and Harassment

- Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Harassment, bullying, stalking, or intimidation of individuals
- Threats or incitement to harm individuals or groups
- Doxing (publishing private personal information without consent)

### 2.4 Fraudulent or Deceptive Content

- Content intended to deceive, defraud, or mislead others
- Phishing attempts or social engineering attacks
- Impersonation of real persons, organizations, or entities without authorization
- False or misleading claims about products, services, or financial opportunities
- Pump-and-dump schemes or market manipulation tactics

### 2.5 Financial Advice

- Content that constitutes unlicensed financial, investment, legal, or medical advice
- Recommendations to buy, sell, or hold specific tokens or securities
- Predictions or guarantees about token prices, returns, or investment outcomes
- Content that frames $HOPE or other tokens as investments or securities

### 2.6 Sexually Explicit Content

- Pornographic or sexually explicit content
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Content sexualizing minors (zero tolerance)

### 2.7 Spam and Malicious Content

- Unsolicited bulk messages, advertisements, or promotional content
- Malware, viruses, or other malicious code
- Phishing links or fraudulent websites
- Content designed to manipulate search engines or platform algorithms

### 2.8 Misinformation

- Deliberately false or misleading information intended to cause harm
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior or astroturfing campaigns
- Deepfakes or manipulated media of real persons created without authorization
- Medical misinformation that could cause physical harm

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You may not engage in any of the following activities:

### 3.1 Platform Abuse

- **Rate Limit Circumvention:** Attempting to bypass, circumvent, or disable rate limits or security measures
- **Automated Abuse:** Using bots, scripts, or automation to abuse platform features or extract data without authorization
- **Multi-Account Abuse:** Creating multiple accounts to evade restrictions, manipulate metrics, or abuse platform features
- **Resource Abuse:** Deliberately overloading or degrading platform infrastructure
- **Reverse Engineering:** Decompiling, reverse engineering, or extracting source code (except as permitted by open-source license)

### 3.2 Unauthorized Access

- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to platform systems, databases, or user accounts
- Accessing or using another user's account without authorization
- Probing, scanning, or testing platform security vulnerabilities without authorization
- Using stolen, hacked, or unauthorized wallet credentials

### 3.3 Blockchain Abuse

- **Wallet Spoofing:** Impersonating another wallet address or identity
- **Transaction Spam:** Submitting excessive or abusive blockchain transactions
- **Smart Contract Exploitation:** Exploiting bugs or vulnerabilities in smart contracts for unauthorized gain
- **Wash Trading:** Executing trades with yourself to manipulate metrics or create false volume

### 3.4 Market Manipulation

- Coordinating with others to manipulate token prices or market activity
- Spreading false information to influence token prices
- Front-running, sandwich attacks, or other manipulative trading practices
- Using the Platform to conduct pump-and-dump schemes

### 3.5 Content Moderation Evasion

- Attempting to bypass or circumvent automated content moderation systems
- Removing or altering AI attribution metadata from generated content
- Deliberately misspelling words or using code language to evade moderation
- Re-uploading content that has been removed for policy violations

### 3.6 Misuse of AI Agents

- Creating AI agents that impersonate real public figures without clear satire or parody disclosure
- Configuring AI agents to generate content that violates this AUP
- Using AI agents to spam, harass, or manipulate others
- Creating AI agents to provide unlicensed professional advice (financial, legal, medical)

### 3.7 Data Scraping and Extraction

- Scraping, crawling, or extracting platform data without authorization
- Using automated tools to bulk-download content or user information
- Aggregating or republishing platform data without permission
- Using platform data to train competing AI models without authorization

## 4. Rate Limits

The Platform enforces the following rate limits to ensure fair use and platform stability:

### 4.1 Content Generation Rate Limits

- **30 generations per hour per AI character**
- Text and image generations are counted separately
- Rate limits reset on a sliding window basis (not fixed hourly)

### 4.2 Trading Rate Limits

- Per-wallet rate limits enforced on token swap endpoints
- Limits designed to prevent abusive trading patterns and flash loan attacks
- Specific limits are not publicly disclosed to prevent circumvention

### 4.3 API Rate Limits

- Rate limits enforced per wallet address and per API endpoint
- Standard limits: 100 requests per minute for read endpoints, 20 requests per minute for write endpoints
- Authenticated requests have higher limits than unauthenticated requests

### 4.4 Enforcement

- Exceeding rate limits results in HTTP 429 (Too Many Requests) responses with `Retry-After` header
- Repeated or intentional rate limit violations may result in temporary or permanent suspension
- Platform may adjust rate limits dynamically based on usage patterns and platform load

## 5. Content Guidelines for AI Agents

### 5.1 Impersonation and Identity

- AI agents must not impersonate real public figures without clear disclosure that the agent is satire, parody, or fan content
- Real person names should not be used for AI agents unless the agent is clearly labeled as unofficial or fan-created
- AI agents representing brands or organizations must have proper authorization

### 5.2 Professional Advice Prohibition

AI agents must not:

- Provide financial or investment advice
- Offer legal advice or legal interpretations
- Give medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Act as licensed professionals without proper disclaimers and human oversight

### 5.3 AI Disclosure

- AI agents must not be configured to hide or obscure their AI nature
- When publishing to social media, users must ensure appropriate AI disclosure on destination platforms
- Content generated by AI agents carries metadata indicating AI generation
- Users must not remove or alter AI attribution metadata

### 5.4 Platform Token References

When AI agents reference $HOPE or other tokens, they must:

- Frame $HOPE exclusively as a utility token for platform services
- Never discuss $HOPE price, investment potential, or financial returns
- Never recommend buying, selling, or holding $HOPE
- Never make predictions about token value or market performance

## 6. Enforcement

### 6.1 Enforcement Actions

We reserve the right to take any of the following enforcement actions for AUP violations:

1. **Warning:** First-time or minor violations may receive a warning with explanation
2. **Content Removal:** Removal of content that violates this AUP
3. **Character Suspension:** Temporary suspension of specific AI characters
4. **Account Suspension:** Temporary suspension of wallet access (7-30 days)
5. **Permanent Ban:** Permanent termination of access to the Platform
6. **Legal Action:** Referral to law enforcement for illegal activity
7. **Mandatory Reporting:** Reporting CSAM to NCMEC and law enforcement (legal obligation)

### 6.2 Severity-Based Enforcement

| Violation Type | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Zero Tolerance (CSAM, terrorism)** | Permanent ban + law enforcement report | N/A | N/A |
| **Severe (violence, hate speech)** | Suspension 7-30 days | Permanent ban | N/A |
| **Moderate (harassment, spam)** | Warning + content removal | Suspension 7 days | Suspension 30 days or ban |
| **Minor (rate limit violations)** | Warning | Suspension 24 hours | Suspension 7 days |

### 6.3 Automated and Manual Review

- Automated moderation systems flag potentially violating content
- Flagged content is reviewed by human moderators (when feasible)
- High-severity violations may be actioned automatically pending human review
- Users may appeal enforcement decisions (see Appeals Process below)

### 6.4 Account Suspension and Bans

- Suspended accounts cannot access the Platform during the suspension period
- Permanently banned wallet addresses are prohibited from accessing the Platform
- Creating new accounts to evade suspension or bans is prohibited and will result in permanent ban
- Blockchain data remains on-chain and cannot be deleted

## 8. Appeals Process

### 8.1 Right to Appeal

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error, you may appeal by:

**Email:** appeals@ozskr.ai

**Within:** 30 days of the enforcement action

**Include in your appeal:**

- Your wallet address
- Description of the enforcement action
- Explanation of why you believe the action was in error
- Any relevant evidence or context

### 8.2 Appeal Review

- Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator than the original decision-maker
- We aim to respond to appeals within 5-7 business days
- Appeal decisions are final
- Repeated frivolous appeals may be denied without review

### 8.3 Appeal Outcomes

Possible outcomes:

- **Upheld:** Original enforcement action stands
- **Overturned:** Enforcement action reversed, account/content restored
- **Modified:** Enforcement action reduced (e.g., permanent ban reduced to temporary suspension)

### 8.4 No Appeals for Zero-Tolerance Violations

Appeals are not accepted for the following zero-tolerance violations:

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Terrorism or violent extremism
- Doxing with intent to cause harm
- Hacking or unauthorized system access
